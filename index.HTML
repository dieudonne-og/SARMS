ABOUT US

ACODES MUSHISHIRO TVET SCHOOL Founded in 1987 by parents association ACODES.

ACODES Mushishiro, Boarding school , Boys and Girls, we always seek to produce competent and well qualified professionals.

Mission The Mission of ACODES MUSHISHIRO TVET SCHOOL is: "To contribute to economic and socio-cultural development."
Award and Graduation At the successful completion of the course, ACODES Mushishiro offer certificate. Our certificates are accredited by RTB and works internationally.

Activities ACODES MUSHISHIRO provides opportunities to participate After School Activities for students at all levels. Joining an After School Activity allows students to strengthen friendships with students who share similar interests.

Application

Admission into ACODES Mushishiro TVET School takes place while students have been approved of having the necessary requirements to become part of the ACODES Mushishiro community.

Applications are welcome all through the year. Interviews are scheduled and organized by the administration and communicated to the parents accordingly.

Application is done to our office in working days from Monday to Friday.

You can also apply online via this website by filling the application form where after the confirmation of your application , an email will be sent to you
